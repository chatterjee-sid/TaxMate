The term “income tax” refers to a type of tax that governments impose on income generated by businesses and individuals within their jurisdiction. By law, taxpayers must file an income tax return annually to determine their tax obligations. Income taxes are a source of revenue for governments. They are used to fund public services, pay government obligations, and provide goods for citizens. In addition to the federal government, many states and local jurisdictions also require that income tax be paid. However, many-a-times, a necessity for the government to function, calculation of Income tax can become a tedious job. The aim of this site is to simply ease your tax calculations as much as possible.
